### 12. App Permissions and Offline Functionality

**App Permissions**

The Code Blue Timer app may request the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required? |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| Notifications | Alert you during CPR timing sequences | Optional |
| Audio/Sound | Play audio prompts and metronome during CPR | Optional |
| Storage/Files | Save and export CPR reports | Optional |

You can manage these permissions in your device settings at any time. Denying permissions may limit certain app features.

**Offline Functionality**

The Code Blue Timer app is designed to work offline for critical functionality:

- **Without Internet Connection:**
  - Full CPR timer and coaching functionality
  - Generate and print CPR reports
  - All core features work without login

- **Without Login:**
  - Full app functionality available
  - Print/export current session reports
  - Data stays on your device only

- **With Login (Internet Required):**
  - Sync CPR records to cloud storage (Supabase)
  - Review prior CPR session history
  - Access records across multiple devices

**Data Storage:**
- **Offline/No Login**: CPR session data remains on your device only and is not transmitted to our servers
- **With Login**: CPR session data is transmitted to and stored in Supabase when you choose to save/sync
